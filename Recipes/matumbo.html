Matumbo

The most delicious matumbo

You'll fall in love wit hthis African dish on the first try.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg goat intestines
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • A fnger of ginger

Steps

  1. Wash and cut the intestines
  2. Boil on medium heat for an hour
  3. Add the spices as it boils
  4. Add thickenner and salt then serve
